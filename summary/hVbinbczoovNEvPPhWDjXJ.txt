 This is a message of appreciation and encouragement to those who are facing adversity, to those who have been affected by loss, and to those who have been providing support. It encourages looking for the good and bad in life during the Christmas season, and cultivating a healthy attitude to deal with life's surprises. It also expresses gratitude to Christ for providing peace, love, hope, and joy, and wishes for a merry and meaningful holiday season and a blessed New Year.
